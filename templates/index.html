Laboratory Rules and Regulations

To avoid embarassment and maintain camaraderie with your friends and superiors at our laboratories, please obeserve the following rules and regulations:

1. Maintain silence, proper decorum and discipline inside the laboratory. Mobile phones, walkmans and other personal pieces of equipment must be switched off.

2. Games are not allowed inside the lab. This includes computer-related games, card games and other games that may disturbed the operation of the lab.

3. Surfing the Internet is allowed only with the permission of the instructor. Downloading and installing of software is strictly not allowed.
